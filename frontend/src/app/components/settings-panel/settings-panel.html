Compression Settings

Video Settings

Constant-quality target for the encoder (HandBrake's RF, like CRF). Lower = better quality & bigger files; higher = smaller files & lower quality. Roughly each +6 halves the size. 18 ≈ near-lossless, ~22–26 is the sweet spot, 32 is small but visibly soft.
Caps the longer edge; only downscales videos larger than this, never upscales.

Photo Settings

Photos are compressed (JPEG) to achieve approximately this fraction of file size savings.
Converts RAW → JPEG and replaces the original. Requires sips (macOS).

Live Photo Settings

Applies to the "Live Photos" media type. The still photo is never modified; only the embedded motion video is acted on (backed up locally first).
